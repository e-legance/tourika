What to expect during the ride

Riding with Greyhound means you are using commercial ground transportation. You could be sitting next to someone you dont know and the seats are not assigned unless you pay an extra fee (See counter agent for prices). In every Greyhound bus, you will have the following amenities; wi-fi, lavatory, power outlets and overhead bins to store your hand luggage.
Keep in mind that since you are using these types of service the drive will not be making stops on the way to your destination, you will have to use the onboard lavatory.
