Life should be an adventure

Nothing is experienced unless it is lived! We bring you and your travel companions to awesome cities in North America for you to create your own adventures. We allow you to have the freedom to choose how you travel.

Daily bus departures

Choose from many departure per day to the most popular destinations from all major bus lines including our chartered buses.

Hand picked hotels

Select from a list of hotels that makes sense for you. We mostly propose hotels that are walking distance from the bus station or a short taxi ride away.

Things to do when you get there

Pick from the most interesting available activities and tours during your getaway. You can add activities to your itinerary to make your getaway memorable.
